# Cendorq Master Blueprint

This blueprint records the final category, product, visual, dashboard, and operator-terminal direction after deep competitor review of Yext Scout, Semrush, the uploaded Yext video, and the uploaded prior Cendorq plus Semrush video.

## Master decision

Cendorq should not become a smaller Semrush or a smaller Yext.

Cendorq should own:

**AI Search Presence Repair**

The category promise:

> Cendorq repairs the public signals that help AI systems and customers find, understand, trust, compare, and choose a business.

The internal operating phrase:

> Make the business answer-ready.

The operating path:

1. Scan
2. Review
3. Repair
4. Control

The main product object:

**Presence Report**

The main public command:

**Run Free Scan**

## Market read

AI search visibility is becoming a real software category. Semrush is moving from SEO into brand visibility across search and AI answers. Yext Scout is moving from listings and knowledge graph infrastructure into AI search visibility scoring, local benchmarking, citations, and recommended actions.

Cendorq should not compete by breadth. Cendorq should compete by repair clarity.

The market pain is not only visibility. The deeper pain is:

> The business is publicly present but not clear enough to be confidently understood, trusted, compared, or chosen.

That is the lane Cendorq can own.

## Semrush lessons

Semrush wins visually because it feels like the product already has the data.

Cendorq should copy Semrush's proof rhythm, not Semrush's breadth:

1. Big clear claim.
2. One input or primary command.
3. Product proof immediately visible.
4. Trust proof.
5. Dashboard visuals.
6. Repeated next action.
7. Authority asset or market index.

Cendorq translation:

- Show the Presence Report early.
- Show Presence Score, Choice Gap, Repair Queue, and Control Snapshot as real product cards.
- Build a Cendorq Presence Gap Index instead of a broad SEO index.
- Add dark command-center visual sections for dashboard and operator seriousness.
- Keep proof before paid pressure.

## Yext Scout lessons

Yext Scout wins structurally because it turns visibility into a score, competitor context, citations, and recommended actions.

Cendorq should copy the logic, not the platform sprawl:

- Visibility Score becomes Presence Score.
- Competitor report becomes Choice Gap.
- Recommended actions become Repair Queue.
- Citations become Evidence Boundary.
- Knowledge Graph becomes Business Truth Profile.
- Monitoring becomes Control Snapshot.

Do not copy Yext's broad module sprawl across listings, reviews, pages, social, search, publisher network, and enterprise data distribution until the repair system is already excellent.

## Category definition

AI Search Presence Repair means Cendorq checks whether the business can be:

- found

- trusted
- compared
- chosen
- repaired
- controlled over time

This is better than generic AI visibility because it creates an action system, not a passive score.

## Product doctrine

### Scan

Free Scan is first signal only.

It answers:

- What may be weak?
- What is unclear?
- What public signal may be missing?
- Is deeper Review justified?

It must not claim complete diagnosis, ranking guarantees, lead guarantees, revenue guarantees, or AI placement guarantees.

### Review

Deep Review is cause diagnosis.

It answers:

- Why is the business not being understood?
- Why may competitors be easier to choose?
- What public signals conflict?
- What proof is weak or missing?
- What should be repaired first?

### Repair

Repair is implementation.

It fixes the highest-impact public signals, including homepage message, service page structure, proof placement, FAQ structure, local pages, comparison language, business facts, schema alignment, and CTA flow.

### Control

Control is drift prevention.

It monitors competitor movement, trust decay, offer changes, broken pages, AI answer drift, stale pages, and signal inconsistency.

## Core product objects

- **Presence Report**: central evidence object with score, weak point, proof, limitation, next move, Choice Gap, Repair Queue, and confidence.
- **Business Truth Profile**: approved facts, services, locations, proof assets, restricted claims, and public language boundaries.
- **Choice Gap**: why alternatives may be easier to understand, trust, compare, or choose.
- **Repair Queue**: prioritized fixes ranked by impact, effort, urgency, and confidence.
- **Control Snapshot**: recurring visibility and readiness state.
- **Presence Gap Index**: public benchmark and authority asset by vertical.
