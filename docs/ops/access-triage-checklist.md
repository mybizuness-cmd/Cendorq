# Customer Access Triage Checklist

Use this when changing customer access.

1. New visitor goes to Free Scan.
2. Returning customer uses the same email from the scan or plan.
3. Unknown email goes back to Free Scan, not an empty dashboard.
4. Provider entry stays hidden until runtime can verify email and open the right customer path.
5. Free Scan return copy stays direct, calm, and useful.
6. Verify Vercel green before the next batch.
