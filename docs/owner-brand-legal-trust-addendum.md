# Owner Brand, Logo, Legal, and Trust Addendum

This addendum keeps brand protection, logo ownership, legal boundaries, crawler readiness, and public trust work attached to owner operations without rewriting the main owner manual.

## Owner checks

- Confirm logo source artwork, creator, files, dates, and ownership or assignment records.
- Keep the Cendorq signal mark consistent across public pages, dashboard, reports, billing documents, support messages, and PDFs.
- Run clearance review before relying on a wordmark, signal mark, or combined mark as filing-ready.
- Use qualified legal review for trademark filing, classes, specimens, refusals, and enforcement decisions.
- Do not claim guaranteed trademark registration, competitor blocking, revenue, ranking, indexing, AI placement, deliverability, security perfection, or lawsuit immunity.
- Keep Terms, Privacy, Disclaimer, support contact, security contact, sitemap, robots, metadata, and structured data aligned.
- Keep public crawler surfaces useful and private customer surfaces protected.
- Keep short boundaries near claims and complete boundaries on legal pages.

## Public trust rule

Trust language should protect Cendorq while increasing buyer confidence. It should be clear, calm, findable, and properly placed, never fear-based or cluttered.

## Logo rule

The current signal mark may be evolved after clearance review if the design is too close to common icon language. The goal is a Cendorq-owned signal that is subtle, powerful, recognizable, and usable across light, dark, PDF, email, dashboard, and favicon contexts.
