# Command Workforce Quality Scorecard

Use this scorecard before a major batch is marked ready for review.

## Category command

Score 0-3:

- 0: Generic work that could belong to any agency or SaaS product.
- 1: Mentions Cendorq language but does not strengthen the category.
- 2: Supports AI Search Presence Repair clearly.
- 3: Makes Cendorq harder to copy by improving proof, control, clarity, or customer trust.

## Customer clarity

Score 0-3:

- 0: Customer cannot tell what to do next.
- 1: Next action exists but competes with other actions.
- 2: Next action is clear and useful.
- 3: Next action is obvious, safe, and tied to evidence.

## Visual command

Score 0-3:

- 0: Surface feels noisy, generic, or visually weak.
- 1: Surface is acceptable but not category-defining.
- 2: Surface is clear, premium, restrained, and readable.
- 3: Surface improves trust within seconds and makes the safest next command obvious.

## Report truth

Score 0-3:

- 0: Claims are unsupported or mixed with assumptions.
- 1: Evidence exists but boundaries are unclear.
- 2: Facts, limits, findings, and next moves are separated.
- 3: The report object improves customer understanding without overstating certainty.

## Operational safety

Score 0-3:

- 0: No clear validation or rollback posture.
- 1: Manual review only.
- 2: Validator, docs, or tests protect the change.
- 3: Validator coverage, handoff memory, and release checks clearly protect the change.

## Batch decision

- 0-5: Not ready.
- 6-9: Needs revision.
- 10-12: Reviewable.
- 13-15: Strong candidate for ready-for-review after green checks.

- Visual review needed:
- Customer-truth review needed:
