# Cendorq Strategic Research Batch 34: Partner Channel

Cendorq may later support agencies, consultants, web designers, local marketers, and service providers, but the core customer flow must stay clean first.

## Partner needs

- partner referral link;
- partner dashboard;
- referred business tracking;
- co-branded reports only if approved;
- partner-safe boundaries;
- payout or credit logic later;
- report access scoped by permission.

## Risks

- partners may overpromise outcomes;
- partners may request raw evidence or internal scoring;
- partners may misuse reports;
- partners may confuse Cendorq with generic SEO;
- partners may pressure customers before evidence is clear.

## Agent instruction

- Partner language must preserve Cendorq doctrine.
- No partner can promise rankings, leads, revenue, AI placement, or algorithm control.
- Report access must be permission-aware.
- Partner routes must not expose customer reports without consent and entitlement.

## Implementation implications

- design partner mode later, not now;
- keep direct customer plan flow clean first;
- add partner terms before any live partner program;
- use partner referrals only when access, billing, and report permission boundaries are ready.

## Final command

A partner channel can help distribution later, but it must never weaken Cendorq's evidence, report, access, or claim standards.
