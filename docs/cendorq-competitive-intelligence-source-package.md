## Competitive intelligence summary

### Yext Scout

Yext Scout shows that AI search visibility is already a real product category. The observed strengths are:

- AI search visibility monitoring.
- Brand Visibility Score / Visibility Score framing.
- Search and AI engine coverage.
- Local benchmarking.
- Competitor comparison.
- Citations, sentiment, themes, and recommended actions.
- Knowledge Graph and publisher-distribution infrastructure.
- Mobile access for visibility-risk checks.

Cendorq should borrow the logic, not the platform sprawl.

Cendorq translation:

- Visibility Score becomes Presence Score.
- Competitor report becomes Choice Gap.
- Recommended actions become Repair Queue.
- Knowledge Graph becomes Business Truth Profile.
- Continuous monitoring becomes Control Snapshot.
- Yext's broad enterprise distribution platform is not Cendorq's lane.

### Semrush

Semrush shows the visual and market-confidence bar. The observed strengths are:

- Big confident headline.
- One clear input or first action.
- Product proof early.
- Dashboard visuals that feel real and dense.
- Strong trust posture.
- Repeated CTA rhythm.
- Authority assets such as AI Visibility Index.
- Broad SEO plus AI discovery platform story.

Cendorq should borrow Semrush's confidence, polish, product-card clarity, proof density, dashboard seriousness, and authority-asset posture.

Cendorq should not become a smaller Semrush. Do not compete on broad SEO-suite breadth.

Cendorq translation:

- Build a narrower authority asset: Presence Gap Index.
- Keep the product about AI Search Presence Repair.
- Make Presence Report, Choice Gap, Repair Queue, Control Snapshot, and Evidence Boundary feel like real product objects.

### Prior Cendorq video

The prior Cendorq surface already had the correct strategic lane: if AI/search cannot see, understand, or trust the business, customers may not choose it.

The weakness was not the idea. The weakness was product proof, visual gravity, dashboard seriousness, authority, and execution clarity.

The current work must keep the original lane while raising the product and visual system to category-owner level.

## Master category decision

Cendorq should not become a smaller Semrush or a smaller Yext.

Cendorq owns:

**AI Search Presence Repair**

Category promise:

> Cendorq repairs the public signals that help AI systems and customers find, understand, trust, compare, and choose a business.

Internal operating phrase:

> Make the business answer-ready.

Operating path:

1. Scan
2. Review
3. Repair
4. Control

Main product object:

**Presence Report**

Main public command:

**Run Free Scan**

## Core product objects

- **Presence Report**: central customer-safe evidence object.
- **Presence Score**: first-state read on findability, understanding, trust, choice, action, and confidence.
- **Business Truth Profile**: approved facts, services, locations, proof assets, restricted claims, and language boundaries.
- **Choice Gap**: why another business may be easier to understand, trust, compare, or choose.
- **Repair Queue**: ranked fixes by impact, effort, urgency, and confidence.
- **Evidence Boundary**: what is known, what is limited, and what must not be overclaimed.
- **Control Snapshot**: recurring watch state and drift prevention.
- **Presence Gap Index**: future public authority asset by vertical.

- Command Queue
- Business Truth Profile
- Evidence Console
- Finding Builder
- Repair Composer

- Audit Log
